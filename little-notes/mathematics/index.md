# Mathematics

Mathematical foundations are essential for understanding and implementing data science and machine learning algorithms.

## Topics Covered

- **Linear Algebra**: Vectors, matrices, and linear transformations
- **Calculus**: Derivatives, integrals, and multivariate calculus
- **Optimization**: Finding minima and maxima of functions
- **Probability**: Foundations of probability theory

## Why Mathematics Matters

Mathematics provides:
- Theoretical foundations for algorithms
- Tools for analyzing and designing models
- Rigorous framework for reasoning
- Language for expressing complex ideas

## Prerequisites

Basic familiarity with:
- Algebra and functions
- High school mathematics
- Logical reasoning
