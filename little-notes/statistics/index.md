# Statistics

Statistics is the science of collecting, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data.

## Topics Covered

- **Descriptive Statistics**: Summarizing and describing data
- **Inferential Statistics**: Making inferences about populations from samples
- **Hypothesis Testing**: Testing claims about data
- **Regression Analysis**: Modeling relationships between variables

## Statistics vs. Probability

- **Probability**: Given a model, what data do we expect?
- **Statistics**: Given data, what model is most appropriate?

## Why Statistics Matters

Statistics provides:
- Tools for data analysis
- Methods for decision making under uncertainty
- Techniques for validating scientific claims
- Framework for experimental design

## Prerequisites

Basic understanding of:
- Probability theory
- Algebra
- Data manipulation
